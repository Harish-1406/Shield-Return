---

## Slide 2: The Problem Statement
* **The Reality:** 9.2 million deaths are registered annually in India. Approximately 12-15% (~1.1 Million) require inter-state transport.
* **The Complexity:** Grieving families are forced to navigate complex, time-sensitive logistics (ambulances, embalming, police clearances, flights) while in deep emotional distress.
* **The Tragedy:** They do this alone, overcharged by informal middlemen, with zero tracking and zero transparency.

---

## Slide 3: The Gap
*Why hasn't this been solved?*
1. **No Transparency:** Families call multiple unverified ambulance providers with no price standard or quality assurance.
2. **Bureaucratic Nightmare:** Documentation (Death Certificates, NOCs) is handled manually with no digital verification, causing massive delays.
3. **No Visibility:** No GPS tracking or ETA system exists. Families wait blindly for their loved ones to arrive.
4. **Cultural Risk:** Religious/cultural handling requirements are communicated verbally, risking severe non-compliance during handoffs.

---

## Slide 4: The Proposed Solution
**Meet FinalPath: India's First Unified Mortuary Logistics Platform**
* FinalPath is a full-stack digital platform that manages the complete lifecycle of mortuary transport.
* **From:** Case registration & legal document verification
* **To:** Real-time GPS tracking, cultural protocol enforcement, and final digital handover summary.

---

## Slide 5: Core Innovations (Key Features)
* **OSRM-Powered Real-Time Routing:** Calculates exact road distances and accurate ETAs for any origin-destination pair in India.
* **Mandatory Cultural Protocol Engine:** Enforces specific faith-based handling (Hindu Antim Sanskar, Muslim Ghusl, Christian Embalming) via digital protocol cards assigned to every driver.
* **Tesseract OCR Document Validation:** Uploaded documents (Death Certificates) are instantly validated using Optical Character Recognition, eliminating bureaucratic bottlenecks.
* **Live Tracking Timeline:** A visual, real-time progress tracker (like a food-delivery app) gives families comfort and exact ETA countdowns.

---

## Slide 6: The Workflow (How it works)
1. **Register:** Family inputs origin, destination, and religious preferences.
2. **Calculate:** System routes the exact distance, pricing, and mode (Road/Air Cargo).
3. **Verify:** OCR instantly validates uploaded legal clearance documents.
4. **Track:** A dedicated Human Care Coordinator and Driver are assigned. Real-time GPS tracking begins.
5. **Closure:** Upon delivery, a structured digital handover report is generated for permanent legal record.

---

## Slide 7: Technical Architecture & Stack
**Built for Speed, Empathy, and Reliability**
* **Frontend:** React 18, Vite, Tailwind CSS (Clean, frictionless UX)
* **Backend Engine:** Node.js, Express.js REST API
* **Database:** SQLite (Lightning-fast, serverless case persistence)
* **External APIs:**
  * *OSRM & Nominatim:* For routing and geocoding.
  * *Google Maps API:* For visual live route tracking.
  * *Tesseract OCR:* For automated document scanning.
